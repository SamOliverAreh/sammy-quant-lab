// Models

Multi-Model
Forecasting Engine

Three complementary approaches — classical econometrics, deep learning, and a hybrid that captures both linear and nonlinear market dynamics.

📐
ARIMA
Auto-ARIMA with AIC-based order selection across all (p,d,q) combinations. Returns 95% confidence intervals for forecast uncertainty quantification.
statsmodels AIC selection conf. intervals
🧠
LSTM
PyTorch 2-layer stacked LSTM with dropout, gradient clipping, and StepLR scheduler. Configurable hidden units and look-back window.
PyTorch dropout LR scheduler
⚡
Hybrid
ARIMA captures linear structure. LSTM learns nonlinear residuals. Final forecast combines both for comprehensive modelling.
decomposition residual learning
🌊
GARCH(1,1)
Volatility clustering modelling for risk assessment. 10-day forward volatility forecast alongside price predictions.
arch vol forecast risk metrics
