12 Forecasting Models

From classical econometrics to transformer attention — each model targets a different regime of market behaviour.

Statistical
ARIMA
Auto order selection via AIC. Best for near-stationary series and short-horizon FX.
95% confidence intervals
Statistical
ETS · Holt Trend
Exponential smoothing with damped trend. Robust to trending and seasonal series.
Damped trend · AIC optimised
Statistical
Prophet
Meta's Prophet — handles holidays, changepoints and multi-scale seasonality natively.
95% uncertainty bands
Statistical
GARCH(1,1)
Volatility clustering model. Produces forward volatility forecasts for risk budgeting.
10-day vol forecast
Deep Learning
LSTM
Stacked 2-layer LSTM with dropout, gradient clipping and LR scheduling (PyTorch).
Configurable hidden units
Deep Learning
GRU
Gated Recurrent Unit — lighter than LSTM, often converges faster on financial series.
2-layer · gradient clipping
Deep Learning
Transformer
Encoder-only Transformer with positional encoding and multi-head self-attention.
Attention-based sequence model
Tree Models
XGBoost
Gradient-boosted trees on recursive lag + rolling stat features. Fast and interpretable.
n_estimators=300
Tree Models
Random Forest
Bootstrap-aggregated regression trees. Good baseline variance reducer.
n_estimators=200 · parallel
Hybrid
ARIMA + LSTM
ARIMA captures linear structure; LSTM learns the nonlinear residuals.
Decomposition approach
Hybrid
ETS + GRU
Holt-trend baseline extracts the trend signal; GRU corrects nonlinear deviations.
Trend + residual split
Hybrid
Prophet + XGBoost
Prophet decomposes trend & seasonality; XGBoost fits the remaining residuals.
Seasonality + ML residuals
🏆 Ensemble
Stacked Ensemble
Walk-forward cross-validation generates meta-training data from 6 base models (ARIMA, ETS, LSTM, GRU, XGBoost, RF). A Ridge regression meta-learner learns the optimal weighting — no look-ahead bias.
Ridge meta-learner · 6 base models · OOS stacking

Evaluation Framework

6 Performance Metrics

RMSE
Root Mean Squared Error — heavily penalises large deviations
MAE
Mean Absolute Error — average absolute price deviation
MAPE
Mean Absolute % Error — relative accuracy across different price scales
Direction Accuracy
% correct up/down calls. >55% is meaningful; 50% = random
Sharpe Ratio
Risk-adjusted return (annualised, 252 trading days)
Max Drawdown
Largest peak-to-trough loss observed in the price series
📚 Malaysia Financial Education · Pendidikan Kewangan Malaysia

Built for Malaysian Investors & Analysts

Understanding model output in the context of Malaysian markets. Memahami output model dalam konteks pasaran Malaysia.

🏦
Bank Negara & USDMYR
BNM monetary policy directly affects the ringgit. Models cannot predict policy decisions — always check BNM announcements.
Dasar monetari BNM mempengaruhi nilai ringgit secara langsung.
🛢
Oil & Palm Oil (CPO)
Malaysia is a net oil exporter. Crude and CPO prices are key macro drivers for USDMYR, KLCI, and energy stocks.
Harga minyak mentah dan CPO mempengaruhi USDMYR dan KLCI.
🇨🇳
China Correlation
KLCI and MYR are heavily correlated with Chinese economic data — Hang Seng and CNY are useful leading indicators.
KLCI dan MYR berkait rapat dengan ekonomi China.
📉
Interpreting Direction Accuracy
50% = random guess. A model above 55% consistently has predictive edge. Use this metric more than RMSE for trading signals.
Ketepatan arah >55% menunjukkan kelebihan ramalan yang bermakna.
💰
Bursa Stocks — Dividends Matter
Price-only models miss dividend yield. Maybank and Public Bank are well-known for high yields — factor this in separately.
Model harga sahaja mengabaikan hasil dividen — pertimbangkan secara berasingan.
⚠️
Model Limitations
No ML model captures geopolitical risk, BNM intervention, or surprise macro events. Always layer qualitative analysis on top.
Model ML tidak dapat meramalkan risiko geopolitik atau intervensi BNM.
