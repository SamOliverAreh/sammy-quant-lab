Statistical Validation

5 Pre-Modelling Tests

Every dataset is tested before any model runs — results guide model selection and risk assumptions.

ADF — Augmented Dickey-Fuller
H₀: Series has a unit root (non-stationary)
Reject H₀ → stationary. Log-returns are typically stationary, confirming suitability for time-series modelling.
Stationarity
KPSS
H₀: Series IS stationary (complement to ADF)
Fail to reject H₀ → stationary. Used together with ADF: if both agree, stationarity is confirmed strongly.
Stationarity
Ljung-Box
H₀: No serial autocorrelation up to lag k
Reject H₀ → autocorrelation present → AR/MA or LSTM models can exploit this structure. No rejection → near white noise, favour nonlinear models.
Autocorrelation
Jarque-Bera
H₀: Returns are normally distributed
Reject H₀ → fat tails / skewness present. Standard Gaussian VaR underestimates tail risk. Use Student-t GARCH or CVaR-based risk measures.
Normality
ARCH-LM
H₀: No conditional heteroscedasticity (ARCH effects)
Reject H₀ → volatility clustering confirmed. Large moves follow large moves — GARCH modelling is warranted and GARCH-based position sizing is recommended.
Volatility Clustering

Root Mean Squared Error on log-returns. Penalises large deviations more heavily than MAE.
MAE
Mean Absolute Error on log-returns. Robust to outliers.

Mean Absolute Percentage Error. Relative accuracy — comparable across asset classes with different scales.

📚 Pendidikan Kewangan Malaysia

Built for Malaysian Investors & Analysts

Interpreting log-return models in the context of Malaysian markets.

📉
Why Log-Returns?
Log-returns are additive, stationary, and unit-free — making them directly comparable between USDMYR and BTC. Raw price levels are non-stationary and mislead models.
Pulangan log adalah pegun dan boleh dibandingkan merentasi semua aset.
🏦
BNM & USDMYR
Bank Negara Malaysia policy directly moves the ringgit. No statistical test can predict BNM intervention. Always layer macro analysis on top of model output.
Dasar BNM mempengaruhi MYR secara langsung — model tidak boleh meramalkan ini.
🛢
Oil & Palm Oil (CPO)
Malaysia is a net oil exporter. Crude oil and CPO prices are key drivers of USDMYR, KLCI, and Bursa energy stocks. Granger causality tests may reveal these linkages.
Harga minyak dan CPO mempengaruhi USDMYR dan KLCI — Granger boleh mengesahkan ini.
🎯
Hit Ratio vs Direction Accuracy
On log-returns, hit ratio = fraction of periods where predicted sign matches actual sign. >50% beats random; >55% has practical trading value. This is your key metric for signal quality.
Nisbah hit >55% menunjukkan isyarat arah yang bermakna untuk dagangan.
📈
Sortino over Sharpe
Malaysian financial returns — especially Bursa stocks — have fat tails and negative skew. Sortino Ratio only penalises bad downside, making it more appropriate than Sharpe here.
Nisbah Sortino lebih sesuai untuk pulangan berat-ekor seperti saham Bursa.
⚠️
Model Limitations
No model captures geopolitical risk, BNM intervention, or surprise macro events. The Random Walk benchmark is a reality check — if your model barely beats it, be cautious.
Model tidak dapat meramalkan risiko geopolitik atau kejutan makroekonomi.
